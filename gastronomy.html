Gastronomy & Architecture

Maltese architecture reflects centuries of diverse cultural influences, blending Baroque structures with heavy limestone fortresses built by the Knights of St. John.

Traditional Flavors

Do not miss trying Pastizzi (savory pastry filled with ricotta or peas), fresh Mediterranean seafood, and local rabbit stew.

Historic Landmarks

Walk around Valletta to see the traditional colorful wooden balconies and visit the ancient Megalithic Temples, which are older than the pyramids.
