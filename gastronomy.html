Maltese culture is deeply integrated into its unique culinary style and magnificent buildings. Baroque features meet massive limestone ramparts engineered centuries ago by the legendary Knights of St. John.

Giorgio's Cafe

A premier and essential landmark in Malta's vibrant cafe culture. Located beautifully on the lively Sliema Promenade, it is the absolute best spot to relax with an authentic Mediterranean espresso, traditional sweet pastries, and a spectacular view of the sea breeze.

📍 Address: 19, Sliema Promenade, Sliema, Malta
📌 Reference Point: Situated right on the main Strand waterfront walkway looking towards Valletta.
