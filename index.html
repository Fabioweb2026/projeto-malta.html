Exploration & Adventure

✦ Blue Lagoon (Comino)

Malta's most iconic attraction. A breathtaking paradise featuring crystal-clear turquoise waters and white sandy floors.

✦ Blue Grotto

A spectacular complex of sea caves where sunlight reflections create stunning shades of blue across the water.

✦ St. Peter's Pool

A natural swimming pool carved in a horseshoe shape. The perfect spot for cliff jumping and snorkeling enthusiasts.

✦ Golden Bay & Riviera

Two of the island's most beautiful beaches, famous for their golden sands and unforgettable sunsets, perfect for relaxing.

✦ Crystal Lagoon

Located near Comino, it's less crowded than the Blue Lagoon, offering deep, pristine waters ideal for diving.
