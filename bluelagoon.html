Blue Lagoon

Located on the small, car-free island of Comino, the Blue Lagoon is a paradise famous for its brilliant cyan waters and white sand bar.

Activities

It is the perfect spot for swimming, snorkeling, and sunbathing. You can also explore the small caves around the cliffs by boat.

How to get there

Regular ferry services operate daily departing from Cirkewwa (Malta) and Marfa, making it an easy and beautiful day trip choice.
