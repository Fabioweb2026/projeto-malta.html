The Blue Lagoon (Comino Island)

Tucked away on the small, car-free island of Comino, the Blue Lagoon is a paradise worldwide famous for its brilliant cyan waters, soft white sand, and vibrant marine life ecosystems.

Paradise Activities

It stands as the perfect coastal location for swimming, snorkeling, and sunbathing on the warm limestone cliffs. Adventurous travelers can rent kayaks or book short speed-boat excursions to explore the deep coastal caves and neighboring Crystal Lagoon bay areas.

Transportation & Visiting Info

Since Comino island has no resident cars and only one hotel, you can reach this bay via daily shuttle ferries running from Cirkewwa harbor (Malta) or Mgarr harbor (Gozo). To enjoy the peaceful tranquility of the turquoise water, we highly recommend arriving early in the morning before the tour boats get crowded.
