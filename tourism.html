Maltese Tourism

Malta is a sun-drenched Mediterranean archipelago packed with breathtaking historical heritage, crystal ocean vistas, and dramatic coastal cliffs. It offers an incredible fusion of summer relaxation and ancient time travel.

Valletta: The Fortress Capital

Built entirely by the Knights of St. John in the 16th century, Valletta stands proud as a UNESCO World Heritage site. Walking through its steep streets rewards travelers with dramatic overlooking views of the Grand Harbour, military fortresses, and beautiful limestone balconies.

Top Destinations & Hidden Gems

From silent medieval walking alleys to sandy coastal getaways, discover the unique locations that make Malta unforgettable.

Mdina: The Silent City

A beautiful medieval walled settlement perched on a hill. Explore peaceful narrow stone alleys, golden palaces, and centuries of preserved aristocratic charm.

Scenic Coastal Cliffs

Witness rugged limestone drop-offs meeting the deep blue sea. Perfect viewpoints for spectacular Mediterranean sunsets and hiking paths.

Golden Bay Beach

One of Malta's most popular sandy beach bays. An ideal destination for sunbathing, clear shallow water swimming, and dynamic ocean activities along the northwest coast line.
