Tourism in Malta

Malta is an archipelago packed with rich historical heritage and breathtaking sea views. From ancient medieval fortresses to open natural wonders, it offers an unforgettable summer and historical travel experience.

Top Destinations & Hidden Gems

Step back in time inside the silent walled city of Mdina, stroll through the bustling streets of Valletta, and check out the traditional colorful fishing boats in Marsaxlokk village. For nature lovers, the dramatic cliffs of Dingli offer incredible sunset locations.

Essential Travel Tips

The peak travel season runs from May to October when the weather is perfect for ocean cruises. The island uses type G British plugs, driving is on the left side of the road, and local English is widely spoken alongside Maltese everywhere.
