Maltese Tourism

Malta is a sun-drenched Mediterranean archipelago packed with breathtaking historical heritage, crystal ocean vistas, and dramatic coastal cliffs. It offers an incredible fusion of summer relaxation and ancient time travel.

St. Julian's & Gżira: The Vibrant Harbors

Renowned for their lively promenade walkways, traditional boats, and modern yachts, the coastal areas of St. Julian's and Gżira represent the energetic pulse of Malta. It is the absolute premier destination for stunning night views, exceptional harborside dining, and scenic seaside walks.

Top Destinations & Hidden Gems

From silent medieval walking alleys to sandy coastal getaways, discover the unique locations that make Malta unforgettable.

Mdina: The Silent City

A beautiful medieval walled settlement perched on a hill. Explore peaceful narrow stone alleys, golden palaces, and centuries of preserved aristocratic charm.

Riviera Bay Beach

One of Malta's most popular panoramic beach bays. An ideal destination for sunbathing, clear shallow water swimming, and viewing scenic coastal cliffs at sunset.

St. Julian's Promenade

Walk along the extensive coastal front line, boasting beautiful views of traditional colored boats and the vibrant urban skyline architecture across the water.
