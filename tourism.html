Tourism in Malta

Malta is an archipelago packed with rich history and breathtaking landscapes. From ancient walled cities to open crystal bays, there is something for every traveler.

Top Attractions

Visit the historic city of Valletta, explore the silent streets of Mdina, and take a boat ride to Gozo Island.

Travel Information

The best time to visit is between May and October. Public transportation connects all major historical landmarks effectively.
