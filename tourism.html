Tourism in Malta

Explore historic walled cities, ancient paths, and scenic viewpoint routes across the islands.

Top Historical Cities

Valletta

The majestic capital city, a UNESCO World Heritage site packed with Baroque architecture, historic forts, and deep harbors.

Mdina

Known as the "Silent City," this ancient medieval walled capital offers peaceful narrow streets and breathtaking island views.

The Three Cities

Vittoriosa, Senglea, and Cospicua offer a genuine slice of Maltese history and gorgeous waterfront marinas.

Scenic Routes & Trails

Dingli Cliffs

The highest point of Malta, offering dramatic coastal views and unforgettable Mediterranean sunset vantage points.

Gozo Coastal Walks

Explore hidden paths, salt pans, and rugged cliffside views around Malta's quieter sister island.
